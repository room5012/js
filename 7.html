What is a yurt?
Our locury yurts are permanent four feet off the ground. Each has canvas wall, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts fornished
Each yurt is fornished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gsa-fire stove. The Luxmy camping also includes electricity and a sink hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom faclities are located in lodge.

What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some to relax! Most gaests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layer of clothing.
